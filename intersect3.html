Annika

Annika Luk grew up knowing she was not going to cure cancer or pursue a battle of wits in a court house like everyone said she should, she was going focus her passion in the arts. With support from her amazing parents and a knack for the Pen tool, her career began in early high school. Now, not just a designer but an innovator, Annika explores the line between fine arts and graphic design and finds her niche in the realm where they coexist. While you may not find her working at an office in the future, search your local art museums and galleries to say hello and bring snacks bc she definitely overslept..
